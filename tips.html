Study Strategies & Expert Tips

Use these research-backed strategies to get the most out of your Versant practice sessions and maximise your score on assessment day.

1. Build a Daily Practice Habit

Consistency outperforms intensity. A 20-minute daily session beats a two-hour weekend session every time. Use the streak tracker to hold yourself accountable.

2. Read Aloud — Technique Over Speed

The most common mistake in Read Aloud is rushing. Focus on articulating each word clearly, pausing naturally at commas and full stops, and maintaining a steady rhythm.

3. Listen & Type — Build Sentence Memory

Resist the urge to type while the audio is playing. Instead, listen to the complete sentence, form a mental image of the full structure, then type from memory.

4. Typing Accuracy Comes Before Speed

Practise without looking at the keyboard — even if it slows you down initially. Aim for 95%+ accuracy first. Speed is a natural byproduct of accuracy once muscle memory is trained.
