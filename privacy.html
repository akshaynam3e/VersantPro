Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This policy explains how Versant Practice PRO collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to provide the service:

  • Your display name and email address (used for account creation and login)
  • Practice progress, XP, and streak history (used to sync your learning journey)
  • Daily activity logs (used to generate calendar and analytics views)

How We Store Your Data

All user data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, a fully managed cloud database service. A local backup is also maintained in your browser's local storage.

Third-Party Services & AdSense Compliance

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and data storage.
  • GNews API: Used to fetch public news headlines.
  • Google AdSense: Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
